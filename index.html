RAG

Retrieval Strategy Optimization for Multi-turn Legal Consultation Systems

Undergraduate Thesis - Jan. 2026 - May 2026

Designed a RAG-based legal document retrieval optimization framework for multi-turn Chinese legal consultation, addressing context dependency, information omission, colloquial queries, and formal legal expression mismatch.

Built an evaluation pipeline on LexRAG covering 17,228 Chinese legal articles and 5,065 multi-turn consultation queries. The HyDE-enhanced hybrid retrieval strategy improved Recall@10 from 0.3757 to 0.4701 and NDCG@10 from 0.2651 to 0.3349.
